Thai Peanut-Lime Sauce
Copilot


2 tbsp. peanut butter 1 large grated garlic clove
2 tbsp. soy sauce pinch of chili flakes
2 tbsp. lime juice
2 tsp. brown sugar or honey

1. Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl.
2. Add warm water a tsp. at a time until it becomes a pourable, silky sauce.
3. Toss with steamed broccoli, noodles, or whatever you choose.
